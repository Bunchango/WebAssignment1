Love is War

"Love is War," also known as "Kaguya-sama: Love is War," is a popular Japanese romantic comedy manga and anime series written and illustrated by Aka Akasaka. The story revolves around the prestigious Shuchiin Academy, where two brilliant and prideful student council members, Kaguya Shinomiya and Miyuki Shirogane, develop feelings for each other. However, both Kaguya and Miyuki are too proud to confess their love first, believing that whoever confesses will be at a disadvantage in the relationship. Thus, they engage in a series of humorous and elaborate mind games, attempting to make the other person confess first. Their antics lead to absurd situations and hilarious misunderstandings as they plot and strategize to outwit each other in the battle of love.

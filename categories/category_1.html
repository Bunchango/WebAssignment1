Seinen

Seinen is a manga and anime genre for adult male audiences, known for mature themes, complex storytelling, and deeper character development. It explores psychological, philosophical, and societal issues, often featuring graphic content and mature themes.

Vagabond

Vagabond is an epic manga masterpiece depicting the life of legendary swordsman Miyamoto Musashi with stunning art and captivating storytelling

Berserk

Berserk is a dark and visceral manga that follows the tragic journey of Guts, a lone mercenary, through a world of dark fantasy and relentless battles

Vinland Saga

"Vinland Saga" is an epic historical manga that follows the journey of Thorfinn, a skilled warrior seeking revenge and redemption in the Viking Age.

Shounen

Shounen is a manga and anime genre aimed at young male audiences. It typically features action, adventure, and themes of friendship and growth. The stories follow young male protagonists on exciting journeys or challenges as they develop their skills and character. Shounen series are known for their thrilling action and engaging plotlines, appealing to fans of all ages and genders.

Mob Psycho

"Mob Psycho 100" manga follows Shigeo Kageyama, a powerful psychic, as he seeks self-discovery, friendships, and acceptance through supernatural adventures.

One Punch Man

"One Punch Man" manga features Saitama, an overpowered hero, seeking thrill and purpose in life as he effortlessly defeats foes with a single punch.

Love Is War

"Love is War" manga revolves around Kaguya and Miyuki, two geniuses who engage in a battle of wits and schemes to make the other confess their love first.
